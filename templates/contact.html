Technical Stack

Leveraging OpenCV and PIL for image processing, TensorFlow and Keras for machine learning, and NumPy with Pathlib for seamless data manipulation, this system ensures efficient handling of medical images and datasets. The core architecture, based on Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), captures intricate patterns in medical images. Optimized using the Adam optimizer and monitored via TensorBoard, our system delivers precise, timely, and potentially life-saving results for medical professionals and patients.

About This Project

we, A.Prajwal Sri Tej, A.V.S Hemanth Kumar, T.K Vamshi Krishna independently created this Miniproject for the third semester of my Computer Science Engineering program. I developed a brain tumor detection system using my expertise in image processing and machine learning. Through this project, our goal was to devise a practical and efficient solution for early tumor diagnosis, demonstrating my enthusiasm for technology and healthcare.
